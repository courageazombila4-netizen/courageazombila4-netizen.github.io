Bachelor of Sustainable Mining

Introduction to Sustainable Mining

Sustainable mining focuses on extracting minerals in a way that minimizes environmental damage, supports local communities, and ensures economic viability for future generations.

Core Principles

  • Environmental protection
  • Resource efficiency
  • Community development
  • Health and safety

Practice Questions

  1. Define sustainable mining.
  2. List three principles of sustainable mining.

Geology

Earth Materials

Geology studies the Earth, its materials, processes, and history. Rocks are classified into igneous, sedimentary, and metamorphic types.

Rock Cycle

The rock cycle explains how rocks change from one type to another through geological processes.

Practice Questions

  1. What are the three main rock types?
  2. Explain the rock cycle.

Chemistry

Matter

Matter is anything that has mass and occupies space. It exists in different states such as solid, liquid, and gas.

Atomic Structure

Atoms consist of protons, neutrons, and electrons. The arrangement of these particles determines the chemical behavior of elements.

Practice Questions

  1. Define matter.
  2. Name the particles found in an atom.
